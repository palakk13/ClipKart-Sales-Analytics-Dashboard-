# ClipKart-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-


## Overview

The **ClipKart Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using **Power BI** to analyze and visualize the sales performance of a retail business for the financial year 2026. The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards and data visualizations, enabling data-driven business decisions.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of key business metrics, including **Total Revenue, Total Orders, Total Quantity Sold, Average Order Value, and Top Product Revenue**. It also presents detailed analyses of product categories, top-performing products, sales across different cities, revenue distribution, and the relationship between product pricing and revenue.

This project demonstrates the practical application of **Business Intelligence (BI)** concepts by combining data cleaning, data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive visualizations to help stakeholders monitor performance, identify trends, and gain valuable business insights. It highlights how Power BI can be used to convert large volumes of raw data into visually appealing and actionable reports.

## Objectives

- Analyze retail sales performance using Business Intelligence techniques.
- Design an interactive dashboard for monitoring business KPIs.
- Identify top-performing products and sales locations.
- Compare revenue generated across different product categories.
- Visualize sales trends and customer purchasing patterns.
- Support data-driven decision-making through meaningful insights.

- Bread was identified as the top revenue-generating product.
